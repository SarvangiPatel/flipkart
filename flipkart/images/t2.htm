    Product Description
    High Air Delivery

    Featuring blades with a 400 mm sweep size and offering air delivery speeds of up to 1300 RPM, the Orient Wall 49 Electric Wall Fan ensures that your home is well-ventilated. So, whether you're studying, watching movies, or doing household chores, it will surely keep you refreshed.

    Robust Motor

    Built with a powerful motor, this wall fan is designed for long-term use and delivers magnificent performance for many years to come.

    Aerodynamic Blades

    Equipped with aerodynamically designed resin blades, this wall fan circulates air in a large area with air displacement of up to 5040 cubic metres per hour.

    Tilt Mechanism

    Thanks to its tilt mechanism, this wall fan can be adjusted to your required angle.

    Remote-controlled Mechanism

    This fan comes with a remote control which allows you to easily control the fan from any part of the room. Using the remote control, you can select the speed, set the timer, and turn oscillation on or off.

    Powder-coated Guard

    Featuring powder coated guard with a flexible ring, this wall fan is corrosion-resistant and easy to maintain. The anti-corrosive body and blades make this wall fan long-lasting.

    Touch Button

    Each setting or programme in this wall fan features a high-tech touch button for manual operation, which makes it convenient to use.
